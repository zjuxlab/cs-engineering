Denoting Diffusion Probabilistic Model (DDPM)​

  • The differences between VAE and Diffusion

    • VAEs: Learn models by "searching", both pθp_\thetapθ​ and qθq_\thetaqθ​
    • Diffusion models: Learn by "imitating" a fixed forward process qqq

  • MSE Loss

    L=12∫0Tw(t)Eq0(x0)Eq(ε)[∣∣ϵθ(xt,t)−ϵ∣∣22]dt\mathcal{L}=\frac{1}{2}\int_0^Tw(t)\mathbb{E}_{q_0(x_0)}\mathbb{E}_{q(\varepsilon)}[||\epsilon_\theta(x_t,t)-\epsilon||_2^2]dtL=21​∫0T​w(t)Eq0​(x0​)​Eq(ε)​[∣∣ϵθ​(xt​,t)−ϵ∣∣22​]dt

  • Convergence guarantee

    For large enough TTT, the reverse process is indeed Gaussian

DPM-Solver​

ODE: dxtdt=f(t)xt+g2(t)2σtϵθ(xt,t)\frac{dx_t}{dt}=f(t)x_t+\frac{g^2(t)}{2\sigma_t}\epsilon_\theta(x_t,t)dtdxt​​=f(t)xt​+2σt​g2(t)​ϵθ​(xt​,t), xT∼N(0,σ‾2I)x_T\sim\mathcal{N}(0,\overline{\sigma}^2I)xT​∼N(0,σ2I)

Where ϵθ(xt,t)\epsilon_\theta(x_t,t)ϵθ​(xt​,t) is a result of the neural network

DDPM: 200~1000 steps

DDIM: ~100 steps
